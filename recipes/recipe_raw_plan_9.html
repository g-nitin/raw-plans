Recipe Raw Plan 9

Goal: smoothie

Ingredients: 3 cups frozen mixed berries, 1 frozen banana sliced, ½ cup plain yogurt, ½ tsp vanilla extract, 1 tbsp sugar, 1.5 cups unsweetened almond milk.
Instructions: 1. Add the frozen mixed berries, frozen banana (sliced in half), yogurt, vanilla extract, sugar, and almond milk to a large blender. \n 2. Blend the ingredients until smooth. If the smoothie is too thick, add more milk as needed to make it blend smoothly. Serve immediately and enjoy.

Ingredients: 1 1/2 cup raspberries, 1 cup strawberries, 1/2 frozen banana, 1 cup almond milk or oat milk, 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup, 1 1/2 cups ice, Handful of mint or basil.
Instructions: 1. Combine the raspberries, strawberries, banana, almond milk, honey or maple, basil, if using, and ice in a blender. Blend until smooth. \n 2. Taste. If it's too tart for you, add another 1/2 cup or so of almond milk and another tablespoon of honey or maple.

Ingredients: 1 ½ cups unsweetened almond milk, 2 cups spinach frozen, 1 medium banana frozen, 1 cup fruit of choice.
Instructions: 1. Put ingredients into the container of a Vitamix blender in the order listed. \n 2. Start blending on low speed and increase to high. \n 3. Blend on high speed for 50-60 seconds until mixture is smooth. \n 4. Pour into glasses and enjoy!

Ingredients: 1 banana, 1/2 orange peeled and quartered, 1/3 cup Greek yogurt, 1/4 cup water or milk, 1 to 2 teaspoons honey.
Instructions: 1. Roughly chop the banana and orange quarters, then add to a blender. \n 2. Add yogurt and water (or milk). \n 3. Turn the blender on and blend until creamy and smooth. \n 4. Taste, and then adjust with honey if needed.

Ingredients: 1 banana, ½ cup whole milk, ½ cup cold water, 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup, 1 teaspoon vanilla, 4 to 5 ice cubes.
Instructions: 1. Combine banana, milk, water, maple syrup and vanilla in the blender cup. Blend until smooth. \n 2. Place ice cubes in glass (if using). Pour banana milk on top.
